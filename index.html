Nicotine 90-95%

High to Medium Purity Liquid Nicotine

Description: Our Nicotine 90-95% range offers pharmaceutical-grade, high-concentration liquid nicotine, available in both 90% and 95% purities. These premium-grade ingredients are carefully manufactured to ensure consistency, safety, and optimal performance across a range of applications. While the 95% variant is ideal for high-strength formulations, the 90% version provides a balanced alternative with easier handling.

Application: E-liquids, Nicotine salts, Pharmaceuticals, Nicotine extraction

Purity: 90% and 95%

Nicotine EP

European Pharmacopoeia Grade Nicotine

Description: Nicotine EP is a high-purity, pharmaceutical-grade form of nicotine that fully complies with the rigorous quality and safety requirements set by the European Pharmacopoeia (EP). These standards are internationally recognized and ensure the product is suitable for regulated pharmaceutical applications, particularly in the field of nicotine replacement therapy (NRT).

Application: E-liquids

Purity: EP Grade
